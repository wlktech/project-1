Services and Facilities

Breakfast Buffet

In Breakfast, Replicate the feeling of waking up at one of The Leading Hotels of the World, with these breakfast recipes straight from our hotel kitchens. From healthy grain bowls and avocado toasts, to porridge with a splash of whiskey, and donuts dipped in custard, these varied repasts from around the world are a reason to get up and and at 'em.

Dinner

Take your tastebuds on an adventure, as you discover our award-winning restaurants in Myanmar. Wind down with a serene ambience, while being pampered with charming and attentive service. Savour our skilful chefs' creations from the likes of Hai Tien Lo and Si Chuan Dou Hua Restaurant, bound to indulge your tastebuds and delight your appetite.

Swimming Pool

Royal Palace HOTEL Bagan’s outdoor zone, is the perfect environment for relaxation - either by day or by night. Peaceful and designed to offers guests a haven of rest and entertainment, the outdoor zone hosts a stunning infinity pool, jacuzzi, kid’s pool, and cabana complex. These are linked to our terraced ‘BBQ zone’: perfect for evening soirees and sophisticated dining.The perfect vantage point from which to bathe in Yangon’s sunshine, our infinity pool complex is designed to provide unrivalled relaxation in the heart of the city. The privacy of each and every guest is respected by the provision of separate powder rooms and individual shower booths. Guests also have access to our outdoor jacuzzi, perfectly located to relieve tension by day or night. A romantic haven, ideal for couples, our lakeside zone also hosts cabana accommodation and all-day bar and dining services.

Gymnastics

Perched on the edge of Lake in a bright and spacious glass fronted studio, our Fitness Center commands a prime location, inspiring all who come to work out. Pay a visit to get a full-body workout with the latest top-of-the-line exercise equipment. Royal Palace Hotel Bagan's Fitness Center caters for every need of health conscious guests used to the finest in health, beauty and relaxation amenities. Fitted with the latest equipment and located in a luxurious and modern complex with fine lake views, our Fitness Center provides the basis of a healthy and relaxing stay. Our suite of pools, jacuzzi, sauna and plunge pools are the perfect antidote to a long day spent in the city, whilst our fitness center will leave you breathless with its top-of-the-line equipment and unparalleled vantage point of Lake.

Spa

Hera Beauty & Spa Massage shop is in B1 floor of ROYAL PALACE HOTEL Bagan, and it’s luxurious and clean space where tourists relieve their stress with full-body massage and back, shoulder, foot massages, facial treatment, waxing services, manicure and pedicure service. We also treat a sun burn or trouble for better skin and provide you with whitening, anti-aging and mini care. High quality Hera Beauty & Spa Massage service will come up to your expectation with highly experienced staffs’ technique and reasonable price. There are two types of room, one is a private room and the other is a double room. Both types have bathroom, comfortable and neat.

Pre-Wedding

In Pre-Wedding Service, our camera men will do indoor/ In-Hotel Shooting or outdoor shooting as the clients need. We can arrange the makeup-artist and wedding accessories. For outdoor shooting, we support a car.

Ballroom

We offer the finest location for the wedding of your dreams. ROYAL PALACE HOTEL Bagan is with you for a beautiful wedding, befitting an event that only happens once in a lifetime. We’ve prepared the best services for elegant family gatherings or successful business events, such as international conferences.

Car Rental

You can rent the cars to visit the Bagan Pagoda while you are living in ROYAL PALACE HOTEL. There are good drivers to bring you anywhere you want to go in the Bagan. But it is only for local use only. You cannot go outside of the Bagan City.
